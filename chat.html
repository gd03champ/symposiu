Chatbots automates the tasks performed frequently at specific time periods. This enables the employees to focus on the more important tasks and prevents customers from waiting to receive responses. In this event the participants can present their innovative ideas on chatbot.

Rules:

  • Demo is Mandatory

Description:

Participants can describe their ideas on chatbot at the time of registration. After the registration,participants will receive a confirmation mail in which they should submit the working model of their chatbot. The participants will have to present their chatbot in front of the jury during the event. Winners will be selected based on the quality and innovation of their work.
